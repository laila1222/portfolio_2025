My journey

From kitchen counters to code editors, my path was unexpected. As someone who thrived outdoors, expressed creativity through music and painting, and loved social interactions, I never imagined finding fulfillment in front of a computer. Despite encouragement from those around me, I dismissed coding as too technical and isolated for my personality.
That changed during a period of uncertainty when I found myself working in a kitchen—a job that didn't align with my passions. A chance encounter with a simple game suggested I might enjoy programming, planting a seed of curiosity that led me to Hack Your Future's 9-month program. What started as a practical decision blossomed into a creative career I now can't imagine living without, proving that coding isn't just for those who fit the stereotypical mold.

Why I mentor?

I believe coding is fundamentally creative work—a fact too often hidden behind technical jargon and stereotypes. My mission is to help others, especially women, discover the joy and creative fulfillment coding can bring. Having made this journey myself, I'm uniquely positioned to guide newcomers through their own transformation from curious beginners to confident developers.
